Piccolo is an Italian word meaning 'small' or 'small', but this drink is not just a latte. Piccolo lattes are made with a portion of ristretto to three or four parts of steamed milk. They are normally presented in a small glass.

Ristretto

$14.63

Ristretto is a "short shot" of a more concentrated espresso. It is made with the same amount of ground coffee, but extracted with a finer grind with half the water. A normal short shot might look like a ristretto, but in reality, it would just be a weaker, more diluted shot.

Affogato

$9.96

Affogato is an Italian coffee-based dessert. It is served in a bowl of vanilla gelato accompanied by a hot shot of espresso. Some popular variations of the original recipe also include a shot of Amaretto, Bicerin and other liquids and toppings.
